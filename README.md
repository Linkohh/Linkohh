## About Me

I build tools that make systems easier to use and problems easier to solve. My background in hands-on IT support gave me something most developers don't have: a firsthand understanding of how real people interact with technology when things go wrong.

That foundation shapes everything I build. Whether I'm crafting an interface with Next.js, scripting automation with Python, or deploying AI-assisted workflows, I design for the person on the other end — not just the spec. I think in systems, communicate clearly, and move fast without cutting corners.

Right now I'm expanding deeper into full-stack development and AI-powered product thinking. I'm looking for roles where technical versatility and genuine user empathy are both valued.

## Featured Work

### [Fitness Workout Planner](https://github.com/Linkohh/fit-wizardly)

Built to remove the planning overhead that stops people from working out consistently. Users can build personalized routines from a structured exercise library — with full control over target muscles, reps, sets, rest time, and intensity — without losing clarity as the plan grows.

`JavaScript` `HTML` `CSS`

### [Word Count App](https://github.com/Linkohh/Counter-Characher-words-and-others-)

A focused writing utility that removes friction from editing. Gives writers instant word and character feedback so they can stay within platform limits, refine copy, and track changes in real time — no setup required.

`JavaScript` `HTML` `CSS`

## How I Work

The way I work is shaped by curiosity, ownership, and a strong bias toward useful outcomes.

⚡ **Proactive** — I move early, reduce ambiguity, and solve problems before they create friction for others.

🔍 **Curious** — I stay close to new tools and patterns so I can keep expanding how I think and build.

🤝 **People-first** — My IT support background keeps me focused on the user experience, not just the implementation.

🧭 **Adaptable** — I shift between troubleshooting, learning, building, and refining based on what the moment needs.

🧠 **Fast learner** — I pick up new frameworks and workflows by doing the work and staying consistent.

🎯 **Solution-oriented** — I care most about outcomes that are clear, dependable, and genuinely useful.
